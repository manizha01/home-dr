Goal/ Objective

Tadawy Dar Khana offers home therapy at your comfort. This service contributes as a stress-free lifestyle to provide assistance to patients at home. Tadawy dar Khana (TdK) will help those in need of assistance and treatment and cannot go to hospitals because it is not only hard but dangerous as well (considering the current situation in Kabul). TdK is willing to drop in and serve you safely at your homes.

Value

ease, respect and caring, as well as,physical, mental, emotional and social comfort for patients, elders and their family members.

Services

nursing and physiotherapy, general checkups and home treatments.
